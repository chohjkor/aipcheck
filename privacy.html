AIP Check (“we”, “our”, “this site”) is an educational website. We aim to collect as little personal data as possible. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected and how it may be used.

This site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may process traffic data to deliver and protect the site.

Cookies and advertising

We may display ads through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads, limit frequency, and provide reporting.

If ads are enabled, Google may use advertising cookies. Your browser settings may allow you to control cookies.

How we use information

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers that help operate the site, such as hosting, security, analytics, and advertising partners.

Example providers may include Cloudflare (hosting/security) and Google (advertising).

Data retention

Retention periods depend on the tools used (hosting logs, analytics, and advertising systems). We aim to keep data only as long as needed for site operations, security, and reporting.

Your choices

  • You can use browser features to limit tracking (availability depends on device and browser).
