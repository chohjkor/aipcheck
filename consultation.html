Aging in Place Consultation

Request a structured review of your home situation and get guidance on practical next steps for safer, more independent living.

Who this is for: This consultation is designed for people who want help interpreting their AIP Score, identifying the most important risks, or prioritizing realistic improvements.

What This Consultation Covers

  • Review of your current home barriers
  • Discussion of Aging in Place readiness
  • Priority improvements to consider first
  • Guidance on accessibility and safety decisions
  • Clarification of your AIP Score results
  • Practical low-cost improvement ideas
  • Suggestions for next steps and further review
  • Early planning for long-term independent living

What This Consultation Is Not

This consultation does not replace medical, legal, engineering, or licensed construction advice. It is intended as an educational and planning-oriented discussion to help you understand your situation more clearly.

Why This Exists

Many people know they want to remain at home as they age, but they do not know where to start. A consultation helps turn a vague concern into a prioritized action plan.

In many cases, the most important changes are not the most expensive ones. What matters first is understanding where the highest risks and barriers actually are.
