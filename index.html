What this site is

AIP Check is an educational website that helps you understand Aging in Place (AIP) — staying safe, independent, and comfortable at home as you age.

We provide general guidance, checklists, and planning tips. This site is currently under development, and content will be expanded over time.

What you can do here (today)

Start with one of these:

1) Aging in Place Readiness Guide
A structured overview: mobility, access, safety, services, and daily living.

2) What Is Aging in Place?
The concept, why it matters, and the key factors to consider.

3) Is Your Home Ready for Aging in Place?
A practical checklist you can apply room-by-room.

Important note

The information on AIP Check is provided for general educational purposes only. It does not constitute medical, legal, or financial advice.

If you have urgent safety concerns, health issues, or legal/financial questions, consult qualified professionals.
