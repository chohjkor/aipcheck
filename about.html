About AIP Check

AIP Check is an independent informational tool designed to help individuals evaluate whether their current living environment supports aging in place.

Aging in Place (AIP) refers to the ability to live safely, independently, and comfortably in one's own home as one grows older.

This website provides general guidance and educational information only. It does not provide medical, legal, or financial advice.

AIP Check is currently under development and aims to support better understanding of housing readiness for long-term independent living.
