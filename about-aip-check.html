AIP Check is an independent informational tool designed to support awareness around Aging in Place and residential readiness.

The purpose of AIP Check is to help individuals reflect on whether their current living environment may support safe and independent living as they age.

What AIP Check Provides

  • General educational information
  • Self-reflection and awareness prompts
  • High-level guidance based on commonly discussed criteria

What AIP Check Does Not Provide

  • Medical, legal, or financial advice
  • Professional diagnoses or certifications
  • Guarantees regarding safety or suitability

All information provided by AIP Check is for informational purposes only. Users are responsible for their own decisions and should consult qualified professionals when appropriate.
