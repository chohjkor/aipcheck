About AIP Check

AIP Check is an educational website focused on Aging in Place (AIP): living safely, independently, and comfortably at home as we age.

Purpose

The purpose of AIP Check is to make the concept of Aging in Place practical. Many people understand the idea in theory, but struggle to translate it into real actions—what to check, what to prioritize, and where to start.

This site provides structured guidance, simple frameworks, and plain-language explanations to support better decisions about home readiness over time.

What you will find here

AIP Check publishes educational articles and checklists that help you evaluate common factors affecting aging-in-place readiness. These typically include home safety basics, daily living convenience, accessibility, and planning considerations.

Content is designed to be useful even if you are not renovating right now. Small improvements and better awareness can reduce risk and support independence.

What you will not find here

AIP Check does not provide medical, legal, or financial advice. It does not replace professional inspections, diagnosis, or personalized recommendations from qualified experts.

If you have urgent safety concerns, severe mobility limitations, or complex medical conditions, consult appropriate professionals.

How to use AIP Check

Start with the Readiness Guide for a structured overview, then use the Home Safety Check as a practical checklist. If you are new to the topic, read “What is Aging in Place?” first.

For best results, revisit the checklist periodically. Aging in place is not a one-time decision—it is a long-term readiness process.

Editorial approach

We aim to publish content that is clear, actionable, and conservative in claims. When we discuss health, legal, or financial topics, we keep explanations general and encourage professional consultation where appropriate.

This project is under development and will be expanded with additional educational pages.
