Recommended Products for Aging in Place

These product categories are commonly recommended to improve safety, usability, and long-term independence at home. The goal is not to buy everything at once, but to focus on the highest-impact improvements first.

Important note: Product suggestions on this page are for general educational guidance only. Not every item is appropriate for every home. Use your AIP Score and home conditions to prioritize the most relevant improvements.

How to Use This Page

Start with the product category that matches your weakest area:

  • Bathroom Safety → if bathroom usability or fall risk is a concern
  • Lighting & Visibility → if nighttime visibility is weak
  • Entrance & Mobility → if steps, thresholds, or circulation are difficult
  • Support & Daily Use → if routine tasks feel physically demanding

Bathroom Safety

Bathroom safety improvements often provide the fastest and highest return in fall prevention.

High priority

Grab Bars

Stable support near the toilet, shower, or bathtub can reduce fall risk and improve confidence during daily use.

View Recommended Grab Bars
Low-cost improvement

Non-Slip Bath Mats

Slip prevention is one of the simplest ways to improve bathroom safety, especially in wet areas.

View Non-Slip Options
Useful for mobility concerns

Shower Chairs

A seated bathing option may help people with balance concerns, lower stamina, or limited standing tolerance.

View Shower Chairs
Optional upgrade

Handheld Shower Heads

These may improve ease of use and reduce awkward reaching during bathing routines.

View Handheld Shower Heads

Lighting & Visibility

Better visibility can significantly improve confidence and reduce night-time accident risk.

Easy prevention

Motion Sensor Night Lights

These are especially useful along the bed-to-bathroom route and in darker hallways.

View Night Lights
Low-cost upgrade

Brighter LED Bulbs

Replacing dim or uneven lighting is one of the simplest improvements for safer circulation at home.

View LED Options
Task support

Reading or Task Lamps

Helpful for kitchens, desks, and bedside use where stronger local lighting is needed.

View Task Lighting
Optional support

Contrast Markers & Labels

High-contrast labels and markers can improve visibility and everyday orientation.

View Visibility Aids
